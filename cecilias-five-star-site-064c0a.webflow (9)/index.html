The time it takes for a Handyangel to complete a project depends on the project's complexity and the Handyangel's experience. Most projects can be completed within a few hours to a few days, some lasting a few weeks... But, send us your *before* pictures and we'll let you know asap!

We offer a wide range of interior and exterior carpentry services, including installing crown molding, baseboards, trim, and wainscoting. We also offer exterior carpentry services such as building decks, installing fences, and repairing exterior woodwork.
